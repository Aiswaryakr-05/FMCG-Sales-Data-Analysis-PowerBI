# Sales Data Analysis - Fresh Way International Trading (FMCG, Qatar)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project features an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze the sales performance of Fresh Way International Trading, an FMCG company based in Qatar. The analysis covers sales trends, target achievements, individual salesmen performance, and product-level demand to uncover inefficiencies and drive strategic business decisions.

## 🔍 Key Business Insights
Based on the data analysis, the following core insights were discovered:
* **Target Shortfall:** Overall sales performance is low, achieving only **24.45%** of the total target with a significant negative variance of **-3.02M**.
* **Declining Trend:** Sales show a declining trend with a **-15.30%** month-over-month growth, with February outperforming March.
* **Team Inconsistency:** None of the salesmen achieved their individual targets. **Muhsin** was the top performer (achieving 50% of his target), while **Shamsudheen** was the lowest (10% achievement).
* **Product Performance:** **Egg** is the top-selling product by quantity, while **Sardine** shows exceptionally low demand or poor performance. 

---

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations
To optimize sales and bridge the performance gap, the following actions are recommended:
1. **Target Realism:** Re-evaluate and adjust sales targets to ensure they are realistic and achievable based on market conditions.
2. **Product Focus:** Focus marketing and promotional efforts on top-performing products like **Egg** to maximize revenue, while investigating pricing or positioning issues for low-performing items like **Sardine**.
3. **Targeted Training:** Provide structured training and resources to underperforming salesmen to narrow the wide team performance gap.
4. **Standardize Metrics:** Ensure future analysis standardizes unit types (box, pcs, pouch) to avoid misleading conclusions.
